About the Game

What is Mini CEO Challenge?

Mini CEO Challenge is a free browser-based business simulation game designed for players of all ages. You take on the role of a young entrepreneur managing a growing retail empire — from a humble Smoothie Stand to a high-end Luxury Goods boutique.

Each round, you make real business decisions: how much inventory to buy, what price to charge, which promotions to run, and how much to spend on advertising. Random market events — from viral influencer mentions to tax audits — keep every round unpredictable and exciting.

Earn coins, unlock new shops, upgrade your infrastructure, and climb the weekly global leaderboard. Mini CEO Challenge is completely free to play — no download required, just open your browser and start building your empire!

How to Play

Master the market in 4 steps 🎯

Mini CEO is easy to learn but takes strategy to master. Here's how each round works.

1

Set Up Your Business Profile

Create a free account with your email or Google, then choose your business name, avatar emoji, country flag, and your first shop. The whole setup takes about 30 seconds — then you're ready to play!
2

Configure Your Shops

Before each round, set the price for each product, decide how many units to buy, choose a promotional strategy (like Buy 1 Get 1, Flash Sale, or Influencer Collab), and set your advertising budget. Each shop can have different settings!
3

Open for Business!

Hit the button and watch the results come in. A random market event affects customer demand — sometimes great (School Event, Hot Weather), sometimes tough (Rainy Day, New Competitor, Tax Audit). See your revenue, costs, and profit for each shop.
4

Grow, Upgrade, Dominate

Use your earnings to unlock new shops and spend coins on upgrades like Warehouse (reduces inventory costs), Staff Training (boosts sales), and Marketing (attracts more customers). Build your streak, earn badges, and climb the global leaderboard!

Tips & Strategy

Pro Tips for New CEOs 💡

Pricing Strategy

Finding Your Sweet Spot Price

Setting prices too high means fewer customers buy from you. Setting prices too low means thin margins. The key is to find the middle ground — usually around 60–75% of the maximum price — where you maximize total revenue. Watch the price indicator in-game for guidance.
Inventory Management

Don't Let Stock Go Stale

Unsold inventory loses value after 3 rounds and becomes worthless after 5. Always try to sell out, or buy only what you need. The Warehouse upgrade reduces your inventory costs significantly — it's one of the best early investments you can make.
Growth Strategy

The Power of Multiple Shops

Owning 3 or more shops unlocks a 10% bulk discount on all inventory. 5 shops gives 20%, and 7 shops gives 30%. Diversification also protects you against bad market events — if one shop has a slow day, others may compensate.
Coins Economy

Maximize Your Daily Coins

The daily ad bonus (+50 coins) resets every day — never miss it! Watch ads between rounds for +5 coins each. Build a winning streak for milestone bonuses. Use the Mystery Box wisely — it can give massive coin payouts if you're lucky.
Upgrades

Which Upgrades to Prioritize

Warehouse (cost reduction) gives value every single round — buy it first. Staff Training (sales boost) helps most when you have multiple shops. Marketing (customer attraction) is best when your reputation is already high. Upgrade Warehouse to max before buying any other upgrade.
Events & Risks

Surviving Hard Rounds

About 15% of rounds are secretly "hard rounds" where customer demand drops sharply. You won't know until you see the results. Keep cash reserves, avoid buying too much inventory, and use the Streak Shield to protect your win streak during tough stretches.

Frequently Asked Questions ❓

Everything you need to know about Mini CEO Challenge.

Is Mini CEO Challenge really free to play?▼
Yes, 100% free! Mini CEO Challenge is completely free to play. You can play unlimited rounds, access all core features, and compete on the global leaderboard without paying anything. The game earns revenue through advertising, which keeps it free for everyone. There are optional cosmetic items you can unlock with in-game coins earned by playing.
Do I need to download anything?▼
No download needed! Mini CEO Challenge runs entirely in your web browser. Just visit miniceo.fun, create a free account, and start playing immediately. It works on both mobile and desktop browsers. Your game progress is saved to the cloud automatically, so you can continue on any device.
How do I earn more coins?▼
There are many ways to earn coins! Watch the daily ad bonus for +50 coins every day. Watch additional ads for +5 coins each. Earn 1 coin per round you play, with bonuses for high profits, selling out your inventory, and maintaining a win streak. Unlock achievements for one-time coin rewards. Invite friends with your referral code for +100 coins each. You can also win big prizes from the Mystery Box!
What is a "Hard Round"?▼
About 15% of rounds are secretly "Hard Rounds" where customer demand is dramatically lower than normal. You won't know in advance — the challenge only reveals itself when you see the results. This mechanic simulates real market unpredictability and keeps every round exciting. The Streak Shield can protect your win streak during hard rounds.
What is the Streak Shield?▼
The Streak Shield (30 coins) protects your win streak if you have a losing round. Normally, losing a round resets your streak to zero. With the Streak Shield active, a losing round consumes the shield instead of breaking your streak. It's especially useful when you have a long streak and want to protect your bonus coins at milestone numbers (3x, 5x, 10x).
How does the Weekly Tournament work?▼
The Weekly Tournament runs from Monday to Sunday. Your total profit earned throughout the week contributes to your tournament ranking. The top 3 players at the end of the week win exclusive prizes: #1 gets 500 coins + a Mythic Avatar, #2 gets 200 coins + an Ultra Sticker, and #3 gets 100 coins + a Legendary Sticker. Rankings reset every Monday.
Can I play with friends?▼
Absolutely! You can add friends by username in the Leaderboard screen. Once connected, you'll see a friends-only leaderboard to compare your performance. You can view your friends' business profiles, send them gifts (coins or rare stickers), and invite new players using your personal referral code — both you and your friend get +100 bonus coins when they join!
Is my data safe?▼
Yes. Mini CEO Challenge uses Supabase for secure data storage and Google authentication. We only collect your email address, username, and game progress data. We never sell your personal data to third parties. You can request deletion of your account at any time by contacting us at miniceofun@gmail.com. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
Who is this game designed for?▼
Mini CEO Challenge is designed for players aged 12 and above who enjoy strategy, business, and casual gaming. It's great for anyone curious about how businesses work — pricing, inventory, marketing, and competition. The game is suitable for all genders and backgrounds. Sessions are short (2–5 minutes per round) making it perfect for quick play sessions.
