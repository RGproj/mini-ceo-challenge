Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Welcome to Mini CEO Challenge ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or play Mini CEO Challenge, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: Email address and username (required for registration)
  • Game data: Your game progress, scores, shop configurations, coins balance, and leaderboard rankings
  • Profile data: Avatar emoji, country flag, business name, and referral codes
  • Technical data: Browser type, device type, and general geographic location (country level only)
  • Usage data: How you interact with the game, features used, and session duration (via Google Analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your game account
  • Save and sync your game progress across devices
  • Display your username and stats on the leaderboard
  • Enable social features like friend lists and gift sending
  • Send important account notifications (e.g., password reset)
  • Improve the game based on usage analytics
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Detect and prevent cheating or abuse

3. Advertising

Mini CEO Challenge displays advertisements through Google AdSense to keep the game free. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

We also offer optional Rewarded Ads — you choose to watch a short advertisement in exchange for bonus coins. These are always optional and never forced.

4. Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how players use the game. This data is anonymized and aggregated — we cannot identify you personally from analytics data. Google Analytics uses cookies stored in your browser. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure, which uses industry-standard encryption and security practices. Account authentication is handled by Supabase Auth, which supports secure email/password login and Google OAuth. We do not store passwords in plain text.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited information with:

  • Supabase: For database and authentication services
  • Google: For authentication (Google Sign-In), analytics (Google Analytics), and advertising (Google AdSense)
  • Legal authorities: When required by law

7. Children's Privacy

Mini CEO Challenge is suitable for players aged 12 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at miniceofun@gmail.com and we will delete the information promptly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (by deleting your account)
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at miniceofun@gmail.com.

9. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days. Anonymized analytics data may be retained longer for statistical purposes.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the game after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: miniceofun@gmail.com
  • Website: miniceo.fun
